Right now me, my mom and dad live in the house and my sister is in Stockholm studying. On my spare time I usually sit on skype with some friends, playing some games or just chatting. Before I applied for this class I was searching for a job. I had recently graduated from another university, though the job scene was not as good as I had hoped. The University before was alot about programming PLC:s (Programmable Logic Controller).
